Military logistics and supply chain

The Military logistics and supply chain is a comprehensive project designed to streamline various aspects of aviation management, integrating multiple functionalities into a unified platform. It addresses key operational domains such as Real-Time Tracking of Resources Module , Procurement Module, Route Optimization Module , and Supply Chain Management Module.

The system leverages advanced data structures like graphs, hashing, and priority queues to efficiently store and process large volumes of data. Algorithms such as Dijkstra’s for shortest path calculations, Floyd-Warshall for all-pairs optimization, and Kruskal’s for route connectivity enhance its computational efficiency.

The Real-Time Tracking of Resources Module monitors the location, status, and availability of logistics assets such as inventory, vehicles, and personnel. . By combining these capabilities, the Military logistics and supply chain aims to improve operational efficiency, passenger satisfaction, and overall aviation reliability.

It represents a practical application of computer science principles to solve complex, real-world challenges in the aviation industry.
